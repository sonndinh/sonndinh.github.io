Brief Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, at Washington University in St. Louis. I am working toward my degree under the supervision of Prof. Chris Gill and Prof. Kunal Agrawal. My research interests include, but are not limited to, analysis and design of scheduling algorithms and synchronization protocols for parallel real-time systems.
