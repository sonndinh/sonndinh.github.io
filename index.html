Brief Bio

I have finished my PhD in Computer Science from Washington University in St. Louis, in January 2020. During my graduate study, I worked with Dr. Chris Gill and Dr. Kunal Agrawal on real-time scheduling and resource synchronization for parallel tasks. From February 2020, I joined Object Computing, Inc. (OCI) as a software engineer. At OCI, I am working on OpenDDS, a free and open source implementation of Object Management Group (OMG) Data Distribution Service (DDS), which is a standard for publish-subscribe communications targeting distributed real-time systems.
